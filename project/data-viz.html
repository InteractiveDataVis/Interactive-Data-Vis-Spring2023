Where does the population growth come from?

The 2020 US Census Appointment Results( link ) provide us with the states that experienced the fastest and slowest growth or shrinking of US states.

It's not an unreasonable assumption that if Utah was the fastest growing state in the US (18.4% growth from 2010-2019), that it would be the result of large size in Mormon families within the state. Turns out that might not be entirely accurate.

Data source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/finnegannguyen/statetostate-migration-flows-from-2010-to-2019
