Project Plan

The project prospectus listed a number of methods for exploring the causes of Utah's population growth as measured by the 2020 census. As a student project to be done at the end of a semester, I've limited the scope and approach to fit the related time constraints.

Scope

In order to potential explain the state's growth as a result of the effects of public safety, proximity to natural recreation, and possible religious community changes would require the requisition of datasets in beyond the census. Knowing my own proclivities to get lost in research, I decided to limit my visualizations to 2022 census data.
